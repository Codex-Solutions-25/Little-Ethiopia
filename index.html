About Little Ethiopia

“My name is Yeshi Mekonnen. I’m an Ethiopian native, who opened Little Ethiopia’s doors in February 2011. I am very happy to do this business because of all the many wonderful people I get to meet.”

At Little Ethiopia, we are passionate about sharing the rich culinary heritage of Ethiopia. Our food is made from scratch, using the freshest ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations. We invite you to embark on a flavorful journey and experience the warmth of Ethiopian hospitality.

Join us and find out why our customers, Mail & Guardian, TripAdvisor, and EatOut consistently rate us as the best Ethiopian food in Cape Town!

Our Signature Dishes

We make our food from scratch, using the freshest ingredients with the best, ancient recipes. Injera, shiro, tibs, doro wot & vegetarian dishes; you’ll find them all. Delicious & affordable. All dishes are served with traditional, freshly prepared injera.
